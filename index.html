Reference Guide

These sections are for reference only and are not editable. Use them to guide assessment decisions.

Progression Indicators
  • Progression from shadowing to supported delivery to independent delivery
  • Increased confidence in client-facing work
  • Consistent and accurate use of internal systems
  • Proactive communication and ownership of tasks
  • Ability to identify issues and take action without needing prompting
  • Clear understanding of training and accounting onboarding processes
  • Reliable follow-through on agreed actions
Causes for Concern / Probation Risks
  • Lack of progress towards independent delivery by day 90
  • Avoidance of client interaction or low confidence without improvement
  • Poor system usage — Rocketlane not updated, tasks missed, or lack of diary/workload visibility
  • Failure to proactively manage or chase onboarding projects
  • Repeated reliance on others without showing learning or development
  • Lack of organisation, accountability, or attention to detail
  • Not escalating risks or blockers appropriately
  • Not following agreed processes after support or guidance has been given
Review Stage Overview
  • 30 days: Foundation & Learning — mainly shadowing, learning, asking questions
  • 60 days: Supported Delivery — taking ownership with support, reduced reliance on others
  • 90 days: Independent Delivery & Ownership — working independently, managing full workload
